About Me

I’m a computer science student at the University of Southern California. I’m interested in solving problems that enhance safety and improve lives, especially in fields like defense and healthcare.

I’ve had the privilege of working on a wide range of projects across different organizations. I spent my most recent summer as an algorithm developer in the Electronic Warfare and Novel Capabilities Group at STR in Boston. My previous experiences include writing programs to improve computer networking at MIT Lincoln Laboratory, and telecommunications engineering at eSimplicity. Additionally, I was a part of the research mentorship program at Google CSRMP.

Feel free to visit my GitHub . Journals + blogposts can be found on my Substack.
